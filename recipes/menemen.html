Menemen

Menemen is a traditional Turkish dish that is made with tomatoes, onions, peppers and eggs.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and other spices

Recipe

Preperation Phase

  1. For 1 person, we use 3 tomatoes, 1.5 hot peppers, half an onion and 2 eggs. Onion is optional
  2. Then, we chop the tomatoes, peppers and onions
  3. After that we start heating the pan and put some olive oil in it

Cooking Phase

  1. After waiting a little bit for the pan to heat, we will add the peppers we chopped earlier and will wait for a few minutes.
  2. Once they look fried enough, we'll add the tomatoes. And leave them cooking until they are soft. Don't forget to add salt.
  3. Once they are soft, we'll crack the eggs in our pan and will make sure we keep cooking until the eggs are not runny.
  4. Bon Appetit!
